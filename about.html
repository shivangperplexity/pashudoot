About Pashu Mitra

Pashu Mitra is a revolutionary platform that empowers farmers and livestock owners with the power of artificial intelligence. Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use tool for quick and accurate cattle breed identification, helping users make informed decisions about their livestock.

By leveraging our in-house developed deep learning models, **CattleLense** and **Creto.ai**, we aim to simplify the process of livestock management, promoting better health and productivity for rural communities. We are committed to building a tool that is not only accurate but also accessible and user-friendly for everyone.

The Problem

In the Bharat Pashudhan App(BPA), breed misclassification by Field Level Workers (FLWs) is common. This manual judgment leads to incorrect registrations, which reduces the quality of crucial data used for breeding, nutrition, disease control, and policy planning.

The Solution

Pashu Mitra is an AI-powered platform that uses image recognition to accurately detect and classify animal breeds. It uses two powerful models:

CattleLens

Our detection model that filters valid cow, buffalo, or goat images.

Creto.ai

Our breed model that identifies the breed from over 15 common breeds with high accuracy.

Our Impact

  • ✓ Minimizes breed misclassification in BPA.
  • ✓ Creates standardized and reliable data for government use.
  • ✓ Saves time and effort for FLWs with easy, AI-assisted registration.
  • ✓ Improves accuracy for genetic improvement, disease control, and targeted schemes.
