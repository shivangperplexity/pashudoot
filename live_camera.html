AI Models Used

1. CattleLense

This is our in-house developed deep learning model that first detects if a cattle (cow, buffalo, or goat) is present in the image. This crucial step acts as a filter, ensuring only relevant images proceed to the next stage.

Train Accuracy

99.91%

Test Accuracy

99.87%

2. Creto.ai

If CattleLense successfully detects an animal, the image is then sent to this second in-house deep learning model. Creto.ai is a breed classifier that analyzes the image to accurately identify the specific breed of the cattle.

Train Accuracy

98.00%

Test Accuracy

87.00%

Workflow

Image Capture
